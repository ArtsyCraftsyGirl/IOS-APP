HOW IT WORKS ?

What's unique in our clock?

★ Our clock has 12 white stars instead of hourly digits

★ Each white stars represents an hour

★ A star lights up dark-blue to show the current hour

★ A star lights up every 5 minutes in a light-blue to represent every 5 minutes of time that has gone by.

What's unique in our clock?

★ A red stripe or white stripe represents 1 minute

★ At the center of the clock, we have the second's indicator, which is a colored circle that can represent either a Swiss or a sweeping movement. It will be filled until it completes one full rotation, which is equal to 60 seconds.
